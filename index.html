Privacy Policy – Jose Aley Business

Last updated: December 22, 2025

Overview

Jose Aley Business is used to connect to the WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta) API to send and manage messages.

Data We Process

To send messages via the WhatsApp API, we may process:

  • Phone numbers you provide as message recipients
  • Message content you send through the API
  • Message metadata returned by Meta (delivery status, timestamps, message IDs)

What We Don’t Do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not use data for advertising.
  • We do not build marketing profiles.
  • We do not intentionally collect sensitive data.

Data Storage and Retention

By default, Jose Aley Business does not permanently store message content.

Logs may temporarily store technical information needed to troubleshoot (for example, error messages, timestamps, and message IDs) and are retained for up to 7 days.

If you store message content in a database, update this section to describe what you store and for how long.

Sharing of Data

Message delivery and related metadata are processed by Meta as part of the WhatsApp Business Platform. We do not share your data with third parties other than service providers needed to run the app (for example hosting and logs), and only as necessary.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data in transit and at rest, including access controls and encryption where available.
